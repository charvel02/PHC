Q: How can I get a study ID/password to access Positive Health Check?
A: Clinic staff from clinics participating in the CDC Randomized Control Trial will provide qualifying patients credentials to access Positive Health Check.

Q: What if I can’t remember my study ID and/or password?
A: Contact the clinic that issued your study ID/password to provide you your ID or reset password.

Q: What should I do if the intervention freezes-up or stops working?
A: If you are in the intervention, hit the “Refresh” button to restart content on the screen.

Q: How do I get the patient handout created by Positive Health Check if I complete the intervention outside the clinic?
A: Clinic staff will collect the patient handout printed at the clinic and place it in a secure area with your clinic records to be given to you at your next visit.

Q: Who can see the information that I provide in PHC?
A: Authorized clinic staff working on this project will be able to access information you provide in the intervention and link it to you. Other project staff outside of the clinic will be able to view information you provided in the intervention but only by your study ID (your name is not included).

Q: What if my study ID and/or password aren’t working?
A: PHC was designed to be used by patients multiple times but no sooner than 2 months from their last visit to the intervention. If you receive the following messages, a description of the error that occurred is provided:
  • "Study ID and password don’t match”- the password wasn’t entered correctly.
  • “Study ID is invalid”- the Study ID entered was incorrect.
  • Please contact clinic staff for assistance”- contact clinic staff and they will advise you of when you can log in again or activate your session. Please contact the clinic that assigned you your ID for assistance if you are unable to recall the correct study ID and/or password.

Q: I downloaded the app, but how can I join the study?
A: Only a small number of clinics were selected to work with Positive Health Check (PHC) at this time. At a later time, PHC will be assessed for broader use and access by other patients. Stay tuned.

Q: Who do I contact if I have questions about the content presented in PHC?
A: Talk with your doctor or nurse at the clinic that issued you your study ID to address any questions you may have.

Q: How do I access the Extra Info site for more information?
A: The Extra Info site is available for 7 days after you complete each intervention session. You can contact your clinic if you require specific information or assistance.
