Data Usage
Positive Health Check (PHC) uses streaming media to show a series of videos which are part of the intervention. Please connect to a local WiFi network before beginning PHC to avoid potential charges from data usage on your cellular data plan.

Virtual Doctors
Positive Health Check was designed to help people manage HIV. The information included in Positive Health Check was developed with the input of people who have HIV, and doctors who care for them. The doctors shown in Positive Health Check are actors.
